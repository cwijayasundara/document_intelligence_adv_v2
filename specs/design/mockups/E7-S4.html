Limited Partnership Agreement

Horizon Equity Partners IV, L.P.

This Limited Partnership Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026, by and among Horizon Equity GP IV, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, as the General Partner, and the Limited Partners admitted to the Partnership from time to time.

Article 1: Formation and Name

The Partnership is formed under the Delaware Revised Uniform Limited Partnership Act. The name of the Partnership shall be "Horizon Equity Partners IV, L.P."

Article 2: Purpose

The purpose of the Partnership is to make equity and equity-related investments primarily in middle-market companies in North America, with a focus on healthcare services, technology-enabled business services, and industrial technology sectors.

Article 3: Capital Commitments

The aggregate Capital Commitments to the Partnership shall not exceed $850,000,000 (Eight Hundred Fifty Million Dollars) without the consent of the Advisory Committee.

Article 5: Fund Term

The Partnership shall have a term of ten (10) years from the Initial Closing Date, subject to up to two (2) one-year extensions at the discretion of the General Partner with the consent of the Advisory Committee.

5.1 Investment Period

The Investment Period shall commence on the Initial Closing Date and shall end on the fifth (5th) anniversary thereof, unless earlier terminated by the General Partner.

5.2 Harvest Period

Following the expiration of the Investment Period, the General Partner shall use commercially reasonable efforts to dispose of all remaining Portfolio Investments and distribute proceeds to the Partners.
