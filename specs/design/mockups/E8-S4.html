Summary

This Limited Partnership Agreement establishes Horizon Equity Partners IV, L.P., a Delaware limited partnership managed by Horizon Equity GP IV, LLC. The fund targets equity and equity-related investments in middle-market companies across North America, with sector focus on healthcare services, technology-enabled business services, and industrial technology.

The fund has a maximum aggregate capital commitment of $850 million, with a total term of 10 years from the Initial Closing Date, subject to up to two one-year extensions approved by the Advisory Committee. The five-year Investment Period begins at closing, after which the General Partner will focus on harvesting remaining portfolio investments.

Fee terms include a 2.00% per annum management fee on committed capital during the Investment Period, stepping down to 1.50% on invested capital post-Investment Period. The General Partner receives a 20% carried interest on net profits exceeding an 8% preferred return hurdle (compounded annually). The GP has committed to contribute 2% of aggregate commitments to the fund.

The agreement includes standard LP protections including Advisory Committee oversight for fund extensions and capital commitment increases, as well as defined distribution waterfall provisions consistent with institutional private equity fund structures.
